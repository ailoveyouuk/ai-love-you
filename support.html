Shipping & Returns

UK Delivery

We ship to all UK addresses via Royal Mail or trusted courier partners. Most items are made to order and dispatched within 1–2 working days.

  • Standard Delivery: Typically 1–3 working days from dispatch.
  • Shipping Cost: Free for all UK art print orders. For larger hardware or bespoke goods, specific shipping terms will be listed on the product page.
  • Tracking: You will receive a tracking number once your order has been fulfilled.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide to almost all locations. International orders are typically processed and dispatched within 1–2 working days.

  • Delivery Time: Generally 5–10 working days from dispatch, depending on destination.
  • Customs & Duties: Please note that international customers are responsible for any import duties, taxes, or customs clearance fees applicable in their country.
  • Shipping Cost: Calculated at checkout based on destination and weight (from £5.99).

Returns & Exchanges

Every piece is produced with care, and we want you to be completely satisfied with your order.

  • Damaged or Incorrect Items: If your order arrives damaged or is incorrect, please contact us at lewis@ailoveyou.uk within 14 days with photographs. We will arrange a replacement or full refund immediately.
  • Personalised & Made-to-Order: These items are non-returnable unless faulty.
  • Standard Items: You may return unused items in their original packaging within 14 days of delivery. Please note that return postage costs are the responsibility of the customer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are your products made?

All of our products are designed and produced in the UK. Our current range focuses on museum-grade art prints, alongside a growing selection of small-batch designed goods that combine algorithmic design with tactile finishing in our Northamptonshire studio.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Most items are dispatched within 1–2 working days. UK delivery typically takes an additional 1–3 working days. If you require an item urgently for a specific date, please contact us prior to ordering.

What inks and papers do you use?

Our art prints are produced using Epson Claria 6-colour pigment and dye-based inks, known for their exceptional colour depth and longevity. We utilise Epson Archival Matte, Premium Semigloss, and Premium Glossy papers (200gsm–300gsm) depending on the aesthetic of the specific series.

How long will the prints last?

When displayed correctly (away from direct sunlight and behind UV-filtered glass), our prints are designed to last for generations. In archival storage conditions, they can last upwards of 200 years; on display, typically 150–180 years without significant fading.

How are items packaged?

We take great care in our packaging. Art prints (A5 to A3+) are shipped flat in rigid, heavy-duty protective mailers with corner reinforcement and acid-free interleaving tissue to ensure they arrive in perfect condition.
