MAN, DAUGHTER & MACHINE.

At AI Love You, we blend the precision of digital technologies with the warmth of human creativity to create art prints and small-batch physical products with character, permanence, and purpose.

Digital Design. Physical Creation.

We are a small, independent father-and-daughter studio exploring how AI-assisted design, digital tools, and traditional finishing can come together to create distinctive art prints and thoughtfully made physical goods.

From our origins in Lincolnshire, our studio is now at home in Denton, Northamptonshire. We believe it is possible to embrace technological innovation while still creating tactile, lasting objects with real character — from art prints to small-batch goods designed to be used, kept, and appreciated over time.

From generative design to handcrafted finishing, every piece we create is personal, meaningful, and made to last.

Lewis & Eleonora McKinnon | Founders
