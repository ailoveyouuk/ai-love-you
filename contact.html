Partner with AI Love You

We partner with selected artists, designers, and makers to create limited and open-edition art prints alongside small-batch physical goods. Our aim is to bring distinctive ideas into the physical world through carefully made editions that feel thoughtful, collectible, and built to last.

  • Premium Production: Museum-grade printing using Epson Claria inks and Archival papers alongside digital fabrication methods.
  • Edition Management: Limited editions include signed Certificates of Authenticity.
  • Careful Fulfilment: Rigid packaging, tracked delivery, and friendly customer support managed entirely by us.
  • Your Rights: You keep your copyright. You grant us a non-exclusive license to produce/sell the agreed editions.
