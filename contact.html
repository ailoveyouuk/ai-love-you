Partner with AI Love You

We collaborate with selected artists, designers, and creative makers to realise limited and open-edition art prints alongside thoughtful physical goods. Our mission is to translate distinctive digital and conceptual ideas into the physical world through carefully crafted editions that are built to last and designed to be collected.

  • Museum-Grade Production: Professional archival printing using Epson Claria 6-colour systems and premium substrates, alongside advanced digital fabrication.
  • Edition Management: We handle the logistics of limited runs, including the provision of signed Certificates of Authenticity and serialised edition tracking.
  • Dedicated Fulfilment: Every order is hand-packed in our studio using rigid, protective materials and dispatched via tracked delivery partners worldwide.
  • Intellectual Property: Artists always retain full copyright of their work. You grant the studio a non-exclusive licence to produce and distribute the agreed editions.
